Aura Institute

    Aura Institute, founded by Tanishq Shah in 2007 in Ahmedabad, is a premier educational institution dedicated to shaping young minds with a strong foundation in academics and technology. Over the years, the institute has grown into a hub of excellence, offering education from grades 1 to 12 along with specialized courses in Computer Science and Web Development. With a commitment to innovation and holistic learning, With a student-centric approach and modern teaching methodologies, Aura Institute empowers learners to think critically, innovate, and succeed in a dynamic world. Its mission is to provide quality education that bridges the gap between traditional learning and future-ready skills.
